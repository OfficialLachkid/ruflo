# Intake Questions — v2 (Editorial Publication)

Batch into ~3 AskUserQuestion rounds. If the user gives a business name in the invocation, skip questions whose answers you can already infer.

## Round 1 — Identity

Ask together (multiSelect where noted):

1. **"What's the company name and a short editorial byline (3–6 words)?"**
   Free text (Other option).
   Example: "Newman & Partners — Recruitment is all about your future."

2. **"What industry / practice, in one sentence?"**
   Free text.
   Example: "Amsterdam-based executive search for finance and legal professionals."

3. **"Which editorial tone fits?"** (header: "Tone")
   Options:
   - **Measured classical** — timeless, weighty serif (Fraunces / Cormorant), muted palette. Best for law, finance, consulting.
   - **Contemporary restrained** (Recommended) — modern serif (Instrument Serif), warm paper background, one deliberate accent. Best for creative studios, boutique firms, editorial brands.
   - **Warm humanist** — softer serif (Libre Caslon Display), cream + terracotta accent. Best for hospitality, wellness, publishing.
   - **Sharp modern** — condensed serif (Playfair Display + tight leading), higher-contrast palette. Best for fashion, art galleries, luxury brands.

## Round 2 — Palette & imagery

1. **"Pick a single accent color (or say auto):"**
   Options with previews of hex swatches. Options:
   - Auto (recommended — based on industry map)
   - Rust `#B45A3C`
   - Ink blue `#1F3A5F`
   - Forest `#2F4A3A`
   - Burgundy `#7A2E2E`
   - Ochre `#B58A2E`
   - Charcoal `#2A2A2A`
   Other: free text hex.

2. **"3 hero / feature image search keywords (comma separated):"**
   Free text.
   Example: "amsterdam canal, boardroom portrait, professional handshake"

## Round 3 — Content

1. **"List 6 services / practice areas (one per line, `Title — short description`):"**
   Free text.

2. **"Contact details:"**
   Free text lines: phone, email, city, address.

3. **"One featured testimonial (name, role, firm, quote) — or 'auto' to fabricate a plausible one:"**
   Free text.

4. **"3 feature-block topics (each: Title | one-line description | image keyword):"**
   Free text.

## Skipping / defaults

- If any field is skipped, choose a sensible default from `design-system.md` and `industry-themes.md` and proceed.
- Don't ask about typography — it's fixed by tone (see `design-system.md`).
- Don't ask about section list — it's fixed at 9 (see `structure.md`).
- Don't ask about animation — the split-flap masthead is the signature (see `animations.md`).
